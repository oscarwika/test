About Us

Hola! My name is Naari! I've been involved with animal care since I was 15. My love for dogs and cats begun when I moved back to the Caribbean after living a few years in Southern California. Upon returning to the island I was shocked with how many stray dogs and cats roam Puerto Rico, I immediately became involved with volunteering and eventually found myself working with people that needed help transitioning their shy adopted pet into their family.

I'm familiar with sensitive and one on one care for dogs that are shy, experience separation anxiety and need a more personal care.

In the last few years I have also worked with active dogs that help keep me in shape and inspire me to spend more time outdoors! Being away from their environment or/and family doesn't have to be a stressful experience for your pup! Instead I strive in creating a fun and dynamic experience during the entire time of their sitting. They're free to roam the entire home, without being contained to a crate and will enjoy regular outside time in nature. I can also provide allowing them to run off leash in safe outdoor spaces with supervision and approval of you of course.

I'm currently studying Integrative Health online this semester and have much more time and freedom then the average sitter.

On the side I also do photography and would be happy to provide a beach photoshoot of you dog for an additional fee.

Day to day pictures and videos will be provided along with showering them if needed during the stay.
